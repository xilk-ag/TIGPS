Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Welcome to TIGPS TALKS ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our social media platform.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Display name, username, bio, location, and profile picture
  • Content: Posts, comments, images, and other content you share
  • Communications: Messages and interactions with other users

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform, including posts viewed, likes, and comments
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and device identifiers
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, and pages viewed

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process and display your posts and comments
  • Enable user interactions and social features
  • Send you notifications about your account and activity
  • Ensure platform security and prevent abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 Public Information

Your posts, comments, and profile information (except private settings) are publicly visible to other users on the platform.

3.2 Service Providers

We may share your information with third-party service providers who help us operate our platform, such as hosting services and analytics providers.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Data Storage

  • Local Storage: Your login information is stored locally in your browser
  • Server Storage: Posts, comments, and profiles are stored on our secure servers
  • Backup: Data may be backed up for disaster recovery purposes

4.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Control

  • View and edit your profile information
  • Delete your posts and comments
  • Deactivate your account
  • Request deletion of your data

5.2 Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your login status
  • Analyze platform usage
  • Improve user experience

7. Third-Party Services

Our platform may integrate with third-party services. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

8. Children's Privacy

TIGPS TALKS is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@tigps.edu
  • Address: TIGPS Campus, Privacy Office
